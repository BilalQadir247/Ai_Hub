## 📝 About The Project
**Bilal AI Hub** is a powerful, centralized platform designed to streamline your daily workflow. Instead of switching between multiple apps, this platform brings together essential productivity tools like custom quizzes, notes, task management, and link saving under one beautiful dashboard.

---

## ✨ Features

* **🎛️ Centralized Dashboard:** A clean overview of your entire workspace with quick stats and insights.
* **🤖 AI Custom Quiz:** Generate and take customized quizzes to test your knowledge dynamically.
* **📝 Notes Application:** Quick text input with a detailed modal view to save and read your thoughts easily.
* **✅ Advanced To-Do List:** Manage tasks efficiently with sorted lists and local storage persistence.
* **🔗 Link Saver:** Store and organize your important web links and bookmarks in one place.
